I worked as an Athletic Tutor at the University of Alabama, supporting student athletes in physics, mathematics, and statistics (2022–2023).

## Responsibilities
Tutored student athletes in course content across physics, mathematics, and statistics
Reviewed and explained course material in a clear, effective, and engaging manner
Conducted test review sessions to support exam preparation

## Academic Support
Guided students in meeting academic deadlines and coursework requirements
Assisted in developing study strategies, planning, and time-management skills

This role strengthened my ability to communicate complex concepts clearly, adapt teaching to diverse student needs, and support students balancing academic and extracurricular commitments.
