I served as a Lecturer for the Master's-level course FY839: Astroparticle Physics at the University of Southern Denmark (2023–present).

## Responsibilities
Delivered six lectures covering key topics in high-energy astroparticle physics
Designed and led three tutorial sessions based on structured problem sets
Guided students through problem solving during tutorials and graded assignments

## Teaching Content
Introduced students to high-energy astroparticle physics
Developed connections between astrophysics, particle physics, detector physics, and cosmology
Highlighted links to searches for physics beyond the Standard Model

This role strengthened my experience in graduate-level teaching, course design, and integrating interdisciplinary concepts in physics
