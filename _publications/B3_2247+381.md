excerpt: |
  We report on multiwavelength target-of-opportunity observations of the blazar B3 2247+381, triggered by an IceCube multiplet alert for a cluster of muon neutrino events. Despite VERITAS not detecting any significant gamma-ray emission from the source during the time period, multiwavelength observations show B3 2247+381 in a low-flux state in optical, ultraviolet, and gamma-ray bands. The source was detected in the hard X-ray band with NuSTAR. Our analysis suggests that the neutrino excess detected by IceCube is likely a background fluctuation, as it was not accompanied by significant gamma-ray activity.
