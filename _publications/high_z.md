venue: "The Astrophysical Journal, Volume 983, Number 2"

excerpt: |
  In this study, we investigate internal photon-photon absorption features in the gamma-ray emission from high-redshift (z ≥ 3) flat-spectrum radio quasars (FSRQs). We find a hint of internal absorption in one source with the lowest redshift in the sample, providing constraints on the location of its gamma-ray emission region along the jet. The limited photon statistics for higher-redshift sources prevented a reliable detection of internal opacity features in these objects.
