excerpt: |
  We report the detection of very high energy gamma-ray emission from the blazar S3 1227+25 (VER J1230+253) with VERITAS. The detection was triggered by a GeV flare observed by Fermi-LAT. A strong 13σ detection was made, showing evidence for emission from compact regions in the jet. We also found optical and gamma-ray correlations, suggesting a single-zone emission model, and a simple one-zone leptonic synchrotron self-Compton radiation model fits the multiwavelength spectral energy distribution.
